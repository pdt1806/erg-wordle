What makes ERG Wordle different from the original?

  • The word is picked randomly, and is different everytime you play.
  • There are unlimited sessions, you can play as many times as you want.
  • It counts the time you take to guess the word, and shows it when you win.
  • This game is light-weight (with no ads), so it loads really fast.
  • This game is client-side, so you can play it offline by saving the website.
